SwissFEL Bernina Help and HowTos
================================

Collection of important documentation and help for the Bernina instrument at the Free Electron Laser source SwissFEL of the Paul Scherrer Insitute.
The collection of documentations is work in progress and might be missing important aspects and contain outdated information. 
Interested scientists are highly encouraged to contact the beamline staff in connection with beamtime proposals, detailed questions and aspects, and ideas for improvements and developments.
